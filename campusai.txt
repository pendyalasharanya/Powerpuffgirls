Our AI-Powered Smart Campus Companion is an intelligent assistant designed to provide instant and accurate campus-related information to students, faculty, and visitors.
The mission is to simplify access to academic, administrative, and event updates through a conversational AI interface.
It leverages Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to fetch real-time data from multiple campus sources.
By integrating structured and unstructured data, it ensures reliable and context-aware responses.
The system operates within the Education Technology (EdTech) domain.
Its primary goal is to enhance campus communication and digital accessibility.
The project uses LangChain’s RAG module to combine large language models with external knowledge bases.
The LangChain AgentExecutor enables dynamic tool usage and intelligent task handling.
For advanced workflows, LangGraph can be integrated to manage multi-step reasoning and decision flows.
The assistant supports queries related to schedules, departments, events, admissions, and campus facilities.
It improves user experience by reducing dependency on manual information desks.
The platform can be deployed as a web or mobile chatbot for easy access.
Overall, the Smart Campus Companion creates a smarter, more connected campus ecosystem.
